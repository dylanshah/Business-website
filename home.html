About Us


Unilab Kenya Limited was established in the year 2000.

We operate in the following business areas:

  • School Laboratory Chemicals & Equipment’s
  • Clinical Laboratory Reagents & Stains
  • University Analytical Reagents
  • General Hospital Laboratory Equipment’s

The initial focus of our business was the supply of laboratory chemicals for schools throughout Kenya and the neighbouring countries mainly, Uganda, Rwanda and Tanzania, and this continues to form an important part of our current business.

Our basic policy is to maintain quality goods at reasonable prices and give our 100% dedication to customer satisfaction.
